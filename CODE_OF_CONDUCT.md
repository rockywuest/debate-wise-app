# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, safe, and constructive environment for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and assume good intent.
- Critique ideas, not people.
- Use evidence and clear reasoning.
- Accept feedback gracefully.
- Help keep discussions inclusive and productive.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or discrimination.
- Personal attacks, threats, or intimidation.
- Doxxing or publishing private information.
- Trolling, repeated derailing, or bad-faith participation.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or block contributors for violations.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues privately to maintainers. Include links, timestamps, and context when possible.
